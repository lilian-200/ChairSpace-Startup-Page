A customized platform for your furniture. Global smart furniture brand. Est 2021.

ChairSpace specifically designs, manufactures and distributes furniture for individuals. Over the years, our main focus has been on efficient production and excellent quality without compromise on every order (no matter how large or small). ChairSpace has an annual production capacity of 4 million pieces and fully controls every step of our supply chain. We believe that we can consistently provide high-quality, laboratory-tested products at competitive prices anywhere in the world.

Our Story: For more than 10 years, ChairSpace has grown from a small local workshop to the largest chair manufacturer in Asia, with more than 360,000 square meters of production space, 3 factories in China and a global distribution network. >>More

Our Reality: ChairSpace products are sold in more than 900 retail stores and provide furniture for more than 200 hotels and resorts in some of the most famous chain stores in the world. ChairSpace has more than 3,500 employees and annual sales of nearly 300 million US dollars.
